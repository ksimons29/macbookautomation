Week 1: Foundation & Core Features

Goal: Understand Claude's architecture and essential PM workflows

Day 1-2: Claude Basics & Project Setup
  • Create your first Claude Project for a real work scenario (e.g., "Senior Product Partner")

  • Write effective project instructions (e.g., "Act as a senior product strategist specializing in AI/data products")
  • Experiment with the difference between regular chats vs. project chats
Practice Exercise: Upload a past PRD and ask Claude to critique it, then create a project with instructions to act as your product stakeholder

Habits to maintain your Claude mastery

  • Iterate on your project instructions weekly

Weekly Review

Pro Tips for PMs

  • Always start with context: Upload relevant docs before asking questions
  • Iterate visually: Use screenshots and artifacts to stay aligned
  • Think in workflows: Build repeatable processes, not one-off prompts
  • Combine tools: Use Projects + Artifacts + Search together for best results
  • Stay organized: One project per product area or major initiative
  • Learn from outputs: Study how Claude structures responses for your own writing
  • Push back: Claude works best when you challenge its outputs and iterate
